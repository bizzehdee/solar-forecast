Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Solar Forecast is a client-side web application that runs entirely in your browser. We do not operate any servers, collect any personal data, or use any tracking or analytics.

Data stored on your device

Your configuration (latitude, longitude, panel settings, battery settings) and cached forecast results are saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Third-party API requests

To generate solar forecasts, your browser makes requests directly to Open-Meteo, a free and open-source weather API. These requests include your configured latitude and longitude so that weather data relevant to your location can be retrieved. Please refer to Open-Meteo's terms and privacy policy for details on how they handle this data.

No other third-party services receive any data from this application.

Cookies and tracking

This application does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or any form of tracking.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
